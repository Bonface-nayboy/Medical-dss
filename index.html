    Common Health Issues

    Cancer

    A disease caused by uncontrolled division of abnormal cells.

    • Signs: Lumps, unexplained weight loss, fatigue.
    • Prevention: Avoid smoking, maintain a healthy diet, regular screenings.
    • What to Do: Seek medical advice, consider treatments like chemotherapy.

    HIV/AIDS

    A virus that attacks the immune system.

    • Signs: Fever, sore throat, swollen lymph nodes.
    • Prevention: Use protection during intercourse, avoid sharing needles.
    • What to Do: Consult a healthcare provider, follow prescribed treatments.

    Diabetes

    A chronic condition affecting blood sugar regulation.

    • Signs: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue.
    • Prevention: Maintain a healthy diet, exercise regularly, monitor sugar levels.
    • What to Do: Consult a doctor, manage diet, take insulin if prescribed.

    Quick Solutions for Common Problems:

    • Practice good hygiene to prevent infections.
    • Stay hydrated and get plenty of rest.
    • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly.
    • Consult a doctor for persistent symptoms.
    • Wash hands regularly, especially before eating.
    • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
